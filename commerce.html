Bachelor in Commerce

TThe Bachelor of Commerce program aims to build a solid foundation in business principles, focusing on key areas such as accounting, finance, marketing, and economics. The program provides students with a broad understanding of commercial operations and financial systems. It equips students with analytical and decision-making skills, fostering problem-solving abilities essential for business challenges. The curriculum is designed to prepare students for advanced studies or careers in commerce and finance, with an emphasis on ethical business practices and professional responsibilities. Students also gain valuable insights into regulatory frameworks, making them capable of navigating the complexities of the commercial world.

Bachelor in Accounting and Finance

TThe Bachelor of Accounting and Finance program focuses on developing a deep understanding of financial systems, accounting principles, and auditing practices. Students are trained to analyze financial data, manage budgets, and ensure regulatory compliance. The program emphasizes analytical thinking and proficiency in financial decision-making, preparing students for careers in accounting, finance, and auditing. Through case studies and practical applications, students gain the skills to effectively communicate financial insights and solutions. Graduates are equipped for professional certifications and are well-prepared for both industry roles and higher education in finance-related fields.
