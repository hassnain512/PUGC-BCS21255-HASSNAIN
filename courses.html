Courses

Our university offers a variety of courses across six departments: IT, BBA, BBF, Commerce, Law, and English. Undergraduate and postgraduate programs are available, focusing on building strong foundational knowledge and practical skills in each field. Students can pursue specialized degrees and certifications to advance their careers in these areas. Our curriculum is designed to provide comprehensive education, ensuring graduates are well-prepared for professional opportunities.

  1. Home
  2. Courses

IT

Department of Information Technology

The IT department offers courses in software development, data science, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. It emphasizes hands-on learning and industry-relevant skills to prepare students for the rapidly evolving tech sector.

BBA

Department of Business Administrator

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) department focuses on core business areas like management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship, equipping students with the skills needed for leadership roles in various industries.

LAW

Department of Law

The Law department offers programs in legal studies, focusing on constitutional law, criminal justice, civil rights, and international law, preparing students for careers in legal practice or public service.

Commerce

Department of Commerce

The Commerce department covers key topics in accounting, auditing, economics, and business law, providing students with a comprehensive understanding of commercial practices and financial systems.

BBF

Department of Banking & Finance

The Bachelor of Banking and Finance (BBF) department offers specialized courses in financial management, investment analysis, and banking operations, preparing students for careers in the financial sector.

English

Department of English

The English department provides courses in literature, linguistics, and communication skills, nurturing students' critical thinking and analytical abilities for careers in teaching, writing, or media.
