Faculties

University teachers play a vital role in shaping the future of students by imparting knowledge, fostering critical thinking, and inspiring a passion for learning. They not only deliver lectures but also engage in research, PUGC students, and contribute to the academic community. Their expertise and dedication help students develop the skills needed to succeed in their careers and contribute to society. University teachers are often seen as role models, guiding students through their academic journey and helping them realize their full potential.
