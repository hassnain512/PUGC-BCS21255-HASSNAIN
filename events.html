Events

University of the Punjab, Gujranwala Campus is a hub of vibrant and diverse events that enrich the student experience. From academic conferences and seminars that broaden intellectual horizons to cultural festivals that celebrate the rich heritage of Pakistan, the campus is always alive with activity. Sports competitions, debate tournaments, and social gatherings provide students with opportunities to showcase their talents, build lasting friendships, and develop leadership skills. These events not only foster a sense of community but also contribute to the holistic development of students, preparing them for success both inside and outside the classroom.

5th Convocation - PUGC

Wednesday, March 16th 2022 at 11:00 am

The 5th Convocation of Punjab University Gujranwala Campus held on March 16, 2022. During the ceremony 1050 students from Business, Commerce, Information Technology and Law awarded the with the degrees. Gold Medals also distributed among 18 students. Worthy Vice-Chancellor Prof. Dr. Niaz Ahmad Akhtar appreciated the efforts of the Director-General Dr. Naveed Iqbal Ch to uplift the University of the Punjab, Gujranwala Campus and continuous hard work. Currently, more than 5K Students are getting benefits from this Alma matter at Gujranwala vicinity.

Seminar on Seerat un Nabi (PBUH)

Tuesday, November 22nd at 2:00 pm

On Tuesday, November 22nd, a meaningful seminar on Seerat un Nabi (PBUH) was held, featuring the esteemed Islamic scholar Saqib Raza Mustafai as the speaker. Attendees were deeply moved by his insightful discussion on the life and teachings of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), gaining valuable spiritual guidance from the event.

The seminar aimed to deepen understanding of Prophet Muhammad's (PBUH) life and teachings, inspiring attendees to embody his virtues in their daily lives.

Advantages:

Spiritual Growth: Enhanced understanding of Islamic teachings.
Moral Guidance: Practical lessons for living ethically.
Community Unity: Strengthened bonds among participants.
Inspiration: Motivated positive personal and collective change.

6th Convocation PUGC

Saturday, December 24th at 9:00 am

The 6th Convocation was held at the Punjab University Gujranwala Campus on Saturday, December 24, 2022. Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Asghar Zaidi Sahib, the ceremony's chief guest, and Prof. Dr. Naveed Iqbal Chaudhary, the director general, expressed their gratitude to the participants and their families. The VC emphasised the importance of learning about and adhering to Allama Iqbal's life and his Shaheen's philosophy. He urged the young graduates to assume leadership roles. The students received congratulations and best wishes from Dr. Naveed Iqbal Chaudhry. He urged the young people to work hard to better Pakistan's future. Over 500 degrees were awarded to students, and those who placed first each received a medal. The Director General gave the campus development report and expressed his profound gratitude to the faculty and staff at the Gujranwala Campus, particularly Professor Aamir Sohail, who is in charge of the Examinations Department, and Madam Adila Hussain, stage secretary. At the ceremony's completion, guests and officials received honorary shields. Among the distinguished visitors from Lahore were Professor Dr. Zulfikar Bora, Professor Dr. Sajid Rashid Gujar, Professor Dr. Azhar Naeem, Professor Dr. Shehzad Sarwar, Professor Dr. Ehsan Malik, Professor Dr. Mudassar Ghafoor, Mr. Raja Shahid javed, Professor Dr. Amjad Abbas Magsi and Mr Khalid Mehmood. Following the event, a delicious lunch was provided for all of the guests and students.
